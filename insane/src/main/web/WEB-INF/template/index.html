(in) SANE

Creating opportunities for the unemployed disabled population and employers

The main goal of this site is to create a platform to exchange information between unemployed disabled population and employers and turn legislation and initiatives already pledged, known and accessible, not only to all the society but also to the employers. Another goal is to create awareness to the benefits of hiring this population as well as highlighting the importance that having a job has to their family and their caregivers.
